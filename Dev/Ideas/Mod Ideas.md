## Minecraft

**Resurrection Mod(Multiplayer).**
When players die, they do not automatically respawn, they enter the world in a ghost-like state. Other players have to do a ritual in which a multiblock structure is created and the player is summoned at that location.

**Dungeons Mod**

Overhaul current dungeons and create new dungeons for more diverse challenges.
- Graveyard that spawns way more undead at night time. Dig up graves to find loot, break spawners and stumble upon traps). Some graveyards will also spawn a mausoleum that hides a larger dungeon room underneath and will have an evoker that you can fight (is basically the necromancer)
- Overhaul dark oak mansions to look more like mansions
- Pillager strongholds that are larger structures than outposts (and much rarer) and contain more loot and baddies
- etc etc

**Villages +**

Upgrade villages to improve various aspects of gameplay related to villagers
- Improve village buildings to improve villager access to job blocks
- Add barracks building and militiamen job (with accompanying job block)
- Militiamen gear upgrades as different villager jobs are added to the village
(Weaponsmith upgrades the sword; Armorer upgrades, well, armor; Librarian adds enchantments; Farmer and butcher increase health (or something); fletcher improves archers bows and arrows; etc etc)
- etc etc
