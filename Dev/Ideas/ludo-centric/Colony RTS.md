## Overview
Colony Sim where you build up colonies and at any point (maybe after the creating of a town hall), you can appoint a governor to run your colony for you (as a new 'ally' NPC) as you move on to a new colony or back to an older colony.

### Governors
Governor candidates can have set traits, backgrounds and stats that would determine how they might run your colony.
i.e. An appointed governor with the 'Ambitious' trait might be more likely to expand your colony outwards to grow their own power and even rebel if they determine that to be in their best interests.
i.e. An appointed governor with the 'Environmentalist' background might make decisions for the colony that most benefit the surrounding environment, potentially to the detriment of the colony itself

## Interacting with other colonies
You can establish trade routes with your colonies and other neutral NPC villages to build relationships and exchange resources and eventually establish alliances or you could invade these villages to take their resources for yourself at the cost of the trust of surrounding neutral NPCs.

Colonies and villages that are not allied to you can join together to found larger-scale rival kingdoms and become larger scale threats as you grow your kingdom (to maintain the level of challenge)

## Story Mode / Campaign
The campaign can follow the story of the American Revolutionary War either directly or veiled as a fantasy story inspired by historical events.
