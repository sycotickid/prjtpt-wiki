#### Rim-world:

Story on a planet whose orbit around its star mirrors that of our moon relative to earth. Thus, only one side of the planet is only ever exposed to its star.

#### Pest:
A game based on the Borrowers.

You play as a small (humanoid) creature who lives in the walls of a house (perhaps procedurally generated).
You must collect resources, fight other creatures, and avoid contact with the humans in order to survive.
Game may start off in a small nook in the kitchen. Player can collect household objects and/or food/supplies in order to stay alive. Some objects can aid in the expansion of the nook.

**SINGLE PLAYER** AI nook-mates can help you to collect supplies/food or just explore. They can also be given tasks such as growing out the nook, setting up surveillance points to look out for humans and their pets.

**MULTI PLAYER** Fellow player nook-mates can divide tasks amongst themselves to help better survive.

Can have small multi-player ( 2-4 / 6 / 8 ) players live in one house and play in that house
Can have large multi-player ( 2-100+ ) players live in multiple houses (perhaps connected via sewer system) and they all survive together.
Can be made into an MMO type game where NPC nook-mates can provide quests to players. Raids can be made on houses where pets are fought.

#### Subterranean Human Horror Game ([[Subterranean Humans]])
A government facility studying the sub-crust Earth stumbles upon a large cavern that goes into a labyrinthian system of tunnels and caves. As they start exploring, researchers start going MIA and even found killed with huge gashes in their suits (they're wearing protective suits). Upon further investigation, it looks like a creature of the genus: homo seems to have come into the cave hundreds of thousands of years ago and evolved to adapt to the subterranean life.

They have thick fur (like thick arm hair/ not bear fur) for greater touch sense and larger strangely shaped ears for greater hearing. Their eyes are there, but they're very small and they're mostly (if not completely blind), their nose has opened up and become some flat looking patch on their face that picks up even the faintest scents. They are completely pale / white. You can see lots of veins. Their fingers are much longer and sharper to get into holes in rocks to find prey. They can crawl through cave crawl spaces with ease.

#### Realistic Lunar Mission Survival:

You are the astronauts entrusted with the task of establishing Earth’s first sustainable foothold on the moon. - Life-like lunar surface map based on actual lunar height maps captured from orbit.

- Play by yourself or with friends (or single player game where you manage a crew (similar to the Sims or Banished)
