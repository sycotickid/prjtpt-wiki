History of Humanity (Kurtzgazat video)
- Simulation game where you start at the dawn of humanity (Adam and Eve or one of their children) and go through different phases of existence up until a more modern age? Basically sapiens, but different somehow? You go from hunter-gathers roaming the map, revealing it as you go (finding rivers, mountains, coasts, plants, etc that will be useful in the next phase), to proto-farming settlements, where you establish a settlement near a feature that you found in the last phase, into large cities fueled by agriculture etc and so on and so forth. Family to tribe to village to city to civilization
