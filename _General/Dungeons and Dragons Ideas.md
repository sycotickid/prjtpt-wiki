## Quests / Story Points:

- Fight a Merc like Yron Greyjoy (Laughing, Sadistic, Raging, Twin Battle Axes)
- Be hired to poison a competing Lord (GOT S7E1)
	- Explain how his body painfully bleeds out from the inside.
- Get kidnapped by a cannibal tribe to be eaten. (Must escape)
- Save a village from bandits exhorting the villagers for "protection" money
- Man pays team to kill his wife & half-orc bastard
	- Fight man if you refuse or fight village if you accept and succeed.
- Town is tormented by raging ents who are attacking farmers for cutting down
their forests.

### Notes about the World
• Most temples have a paladin who protects
the priest and acolytes of the shrine.


## Locations:

"Bell, Book & Candle"
- Tavern in a waste land where traitors who are banished from a kingdom go before leaving civilization for good.

"Dot & Feather"
- Ethnic Tavern in a Foreign Embassy

## Characters:

**Lightning Mage**: Mage, part of the now destroyed Thunder Guild of Mages. Goes through intense training and study in order to learn lightning magic. Complete training by standing on a tall metal spire and getting struck by lightning. Once struck, they chant a sealing spell and trap the lightning with a lacrama. (*Idea originated by lightning scars*)
