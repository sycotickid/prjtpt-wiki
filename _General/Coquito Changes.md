Rum: only 1 1/4 cups or 10oz

Not that order matters too much, but Papi adds eggs, 1 can of evaporated milk and breaks up the eggs, 4-5 pulses. Then adds sweetened condensed milk and vanilla and blends again.

A gallon jug holds 1 whole batch. Use to ensure consistency across all bottles!

Second batch: start with coco Lopez, then add evaporated milk and cinnamon. Blend to incorporate (4-5 pulses) then add rum. Blend and add to jug.

Mixing too much makes the whole batch too foamy!

When pouring into the bottle, tilt the funnel and pour the mixture such that it cascades down the side of the bottle instead of landing at the bottom, to avoid any bubbling.
